With the need for more robust and reliable software, testing has become a fundamental part of the Software Development Life Cycle and a specialized function in itself. Engineering and IT departments face many challenges with testing their applications and systems that include:

  • Distributed teams
  • Unorganized assets
  • Inefficient processes
  • Low levels of automation
  • Quality and schedule visibility

BTF Pro offers a complete range of Software Quality Assurance (QA) and Software Testing Services.

We offer solutions for:

  • Manual Testing & Test Automation
  • Functional, Performance & Compliance Testing
  • User Acceptance Testing
  • Development and implementation of QA Processes, Evaluation & Audits
  • Defects Management

Centers of Expertise

In our Testing CoE, we exploit new testing technologies, and create customized test environments for our customers.

The CoE combines:

  • Dedicated domain and technology experts at multiple levels, including project managers, leads, analysts and testers
  • Test environments, labs and infrastructure
  • Testing Methodologies from Waterfall and V-Model to Agile or a hybrid thereof
  • Testing Tools and expertise for requirements management, defect tracking, automation, build and release management, etc.
  • Test Processes that leverage decades of test experience and industry-validated processes in test planning, authoring, automating, scheduling, executing, reporting and management.

Managed Service

Our Managed Service offering brings in all the benefits of a Center of Expertise coupled with a business model built around deliverables, milestones and service level agreements.

Service as a Utility

Our Testing as a Utility is the next generation in testing services where customers reap the benefits of a simplified pricing model without the management overhead. This service incorporates various vendor partnerships, BTF Pro's testing processes, and our web based tools and frameworks to deliver an on-demand environment designed to dramatically reduce cost and manage spikes in test activity.
