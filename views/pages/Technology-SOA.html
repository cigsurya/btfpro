A Service Oriented Architecture (SOA) approach enables rapid assembly of web services, shortens deployment time, and reduces risk of failure. This ensures that you keep your organization more agile and profitable. SOA is an offspring of distributed computing, whose greatest advantage comes from the level of reusability going up to 2.5 times when compared to the 20 to 40 percent in usual projects. More and more companies want to change the business process often without changing a single line of source code. So increasingly understand the need for SOA. The most important driving force is the power of interoperability in an increasingly global and heterogeneous business world. SOAs allow firms to align their business needs and IT infrastructure, lower development costs, encourage sharing and reusability of services, complete integration faster, and build industry best practices.

BTF Pro's business integration software consulting practice specializes in Service-Oriented Architecture (SOA), Business Process Management (BPM), Event-Driven Architecture (EDA), Complex Event Processing (CEP), Master Data Management (MDM) and Enterprise Application Integration (EAI). We often use these technologies together to modernize legacy application architecture and support mufti-channel user experiences such as portals, B2B APIs, social media and mobility applications.

Our services create a coherent architecture on which to rationalize and modernize legacy systems, automate and optimize business processes, and improve data quality and accessibility. We create flexible architectures and service-oriented business applications that enable enterprise agility.

BTF Pro has been practicing the SOA approach for more than ten years and we have built systems that include hundreds of business services for Fortune 500 companies. These systems are some of the largest and most complex SOA implementations in the world.

Through our many client engagements and rigorous capture of best practices, we have built SOA methodology, patterns and standards that are unrivaled in the industry. Our SOA methodology covers the complete services lifecycle from strategic concepts such as roadmaps and governance models to standards for development, testing and running large-scale SOA infrastructures. We partner with the leading software vendors in the industry including IBM, Oracle, TIBCO, SOA Software, JBoss, Mule and many others. We have captured and created best practices and standards for these technologies and have a process for customizing these standards to our client's needs.
